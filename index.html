ABOUT SUCLA

Surender’s Center for Learning Accountancy (shortly “SUCLA”) is a Trichy based Education Start-Up. SUCLA offers Coaching for Professional Courses like CA, CMA (ICWA) & CS. We at SUCLA equip students from Tier III & IV Towns to face the examinations with greater confidence leading to their early emergence as professional accountants.
Structured Methodology, Expert Faculty, Comprehensive Study Materials, Regular Test and Feedback are some of the key factors that make us different from other players in the same industry.
